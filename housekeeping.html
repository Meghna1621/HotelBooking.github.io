HOUSEKEEPING

The Housekeeping department takes pride in keeping the hotel clean and comfortable, so as to create a ‘Home away from home’.The aim of all accommodation establishment is to provide their customers with clean, attractive, comfortable and welcoming surrounding that offer value for money. Nothing sends a stronger message than cleanliness in a hospitality operation. No level of service, friendliness or glamour can equal the sensation a guest has upon entering a spotless, tidy and conveniently arranged room.
