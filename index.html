Food Service/ Food Runner

The pleasures of fine dining await you, with recipes from local and international culinary traditions. The menu will be enriched with summer dishes designed to take advantage of the seasonality of the raw ingredients and proximity to the sea. Every day the rich buffet, complete with Live Cooking, offers guests Italian and Sardinian specialities. All desserts and icecreams are homemade and prepared fresh every day.

Refreshment

There is a big pool in the backyard, and all over the atmosphere is relaxed and inviting, with a truly welcoming staff. Food service in a patio in front of the lodge, breakfast on the nice rooftop terrace.Offering a little amuse bouche or free beverage upon arrival to the guests is an excellent way to get them in a good mood after the stress and exhaustion of travel, helping to set the pace for the rest of their stay. The expected welcome refreshment is a bottle of wine, a small cheese tray, a fruit place, crackers, cookies or any combination of the five.

Housekeeping

Cleanliness – it’s one of the most researched items when consumers are exploring where to stay for leisure. It builds customer loyalty, generates positive praise and drives hotel profitability. Regardless of property size, brand affiliation or location, cleanliness is an unwavering top priority for hotel guests. Since cleanliness is so firmly tied to financial success, it is imperative for hoteliers to implement a smart strategy for bolstering their housekeeping operation.

Room Security

Guest room locking systems these days include punch and magnetic key cards which have locks with flash memory and other functions. The system can directly be linked with PMS. Trained security guards working 24-hours every day to provide the best in safety and security for the guests. Safety and Security is always the first priority towards guest service.
